Frequently Asked Questions

What is GameChart

Game chart is a free service that compares and contrasts games based on criteria provided by the user. It allows users to easily access information about their favorite games ranging from sales to ratings.

Who are we?

We are a third party website that takes data and information regarding games and compiles them into a single place. We are in no way affiliated with any of the games we display.

Why GameChart

GameChart was created in order to simplify the process of finding detailed information about the latest games and trends in the industry in a simple user-friendly package. It provides a smoother more streamlined experience compared to the alternatives.
